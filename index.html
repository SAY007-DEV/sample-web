Our Experts headed by

Our team consists of highly qualified and experienced physiotherapists specializing in neuro rehabilitation, orthopedic care, pain management, and functional recovery. Each patient undergoes a detailed assessment before starting treatment. Based on the findings, a customized treatment plan is designed to ensure safe, effective, and goal-oriented recovery. We also provide structured home exercise programs after treatment completion and conduct regular follow-ups to support long-term improvement and prevent recurrence. Our focus is on delivering evidence-based care with compassion, professionalism, and measurable results.
